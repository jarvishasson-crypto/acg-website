Our Story

Two decades of
uncompromising craft.

Founded in 2002, A Construction Group Inc. was built on a simple conviction: that every home deserves to be a masterpiece. What began as a solo operation has grown into one of the most respected luxury construction firms in Los Angeles.

Today, the same family that started it all continues to lead every project personally — combining over two decades of master craftsmanship with a commitment to precision that no large firm can match. Every project we take on is personal. Every detail is intentional. Every home is built to last.

We don't chase volume. We earn trust — and our clients return that trust through referrals that have sustained us for over twenty years.

Amir Hasson
