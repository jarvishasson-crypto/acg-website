Our Story

A father.
A son.
A legacy built.

Founded by Amir Hasson in 2002, A Construction Group Inc. was built on a simple conviction: that every home deserves to be a masterpiece. What began as a solo operation has grown into one of the most respected luxury construction firms in Los Angeles.

Today, Amir works side by side with his son — combining over two decades of master craftsmanship with a new generation's vision for what luxury living can be. Every project we take on is personal. Every detail is intentional. Every home is built to last.
